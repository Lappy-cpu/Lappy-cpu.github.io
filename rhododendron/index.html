Pacific Rhododendron

Pacific Rhododendron is also known as Rhododendron macrophyllum, California rosebay, California rhododendron, coast rhododendron, and big leaf rhododendron. Pacific Rhododendron's ranges in size from 6 to 30 feet (1.8 to 9 meters) tall, though it can sometimes reach 20 feet or slightly more under ideal conditions. Its width typically spans 3.5 to 8 feet (1 to 2.4 meters).
