Harry Potter Series

The Harry Potter series is a fantasy novel series about an orphan boy named Harry Potter who discovers he is a wizard and enrolls at Hogwarts, a magical school connected to a secret platform at King's Cross Station in London. There, he grows up with his friends Ron and Hermione and battles the Dark Wizard Voldemort. Why not learn magic with Harry Potter and his friends, live in their dormitory, and let your imagination soar?

  • Author: J.K. Rowling
  • Publisher: Scholastic Inc.
  • Publication date: July 1, 2009
  • Language: English
  • Reading age: +9 years
