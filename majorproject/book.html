Harry Potter Series

The Harry Potter series is a fantasy novel series about an orphan boy named Harry Potter who discovers he is a wizard and enrolls at Hogwarts, a magical school connected to a secret platform at King's Cross Station in London. There, he grows up with his friends Ron and Hermione and battles the Dark Wizard Voldemort. Why not learn magic with Harry Potter and his friends, live in their dormitory, and let your imagination soar?

  • Author: J.K. Rowling
  • Publisher: Scholastic Inc.
  • Publication date: July 1, 2009
  • Language: English
  • Reading age: +9 years
  • Price: Book set is $81.70!

The Lord of the Rings Series

"The Lord of the Rings" is a fantasy novel that follows the quest of the Fellowship of the Ring to destroy the One Ring, and is the sequel to "The Hobbit." It consists of three volumes, beginning with "The Fellowship of the Ring," "The Two Towers," and ending with "The Return of the King." Want to embark on a grand journey like the Fellowship of the Ring? Embark on a grand journey like this book!

  • Author: J. R. R. Tolkien
  • Publisher: Clarion Books
  • Publication date: September 18, 2012
  • Language: English
  • Reading age: 12 years and up
  • Price: Book set is $25.78!
