About Our team

Fairy Fantasia is a website created with the goal of creating your own characters and creating a place where you can let your imagination run wild.

We've often looked with pity on those who possess a wealth of imagination but fail to fully utilize it, and instead find it strange. We decided to create a website for these people, and for them, we created Fairy Fantasia.

We're here for you. We can help you with anything your imagination can throw at you.

Teams

Lauren Lee - Leader of Fairy Fantasia company

She has been writing and illustrating fantasy novels and arts since 2011. She holds a BA in Fine Art from Washington State University.

She aims to work with her Fairy Fantasia team to create more of her own novels and illustrations, and publish them so that they can be seen by more people.
